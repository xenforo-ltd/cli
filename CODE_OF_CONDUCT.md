# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected behavior

1. Be respectful and constructive.
2. Assume good intent and focus on technical issues.
3. Give and accept feedback professionally.

## Unacceptable behavior

1. Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
2. Discrimination or hateful conduct.
3. Deliberate disruption of project collaboration.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct and may remove comments, issues, or contributions that violate it.

## Reporting

To report unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers via the security/contact process in `SECURITY.md`.
